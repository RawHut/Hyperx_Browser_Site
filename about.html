About the Developer

I want to create a browser that runs at our fingertips—no hesitation. Not on a mission but wish to build an open-source, trusted browser.

Over the past year, I've implemented self-updates, an ad-blocker, full-screen mode, and various internal improvements to make Hyper X better day by day.

Detailed Features

🚀 Hyper X Mode

Optimizes internal settings for seamless gaming, streaming, and productivity.

🔐 Data Control

Manage cookies, saved logins, cache and clear all data locally.

🛑 Bad Site Blocker

Scans links against 1M+ bad sites database to keep you safe.

Platforms & Requirements

  • Closed beta on Windows (open beta soon).
  • Coming to Linux & Android.
  • Min: 4 GB RAM, 1.5 GB storage, 1 GHz CPU (64-bit).

Privacy & Security

Built-in permission manager guards camera, mic, location, etc.

No personal data is collected without your explicit consent.
